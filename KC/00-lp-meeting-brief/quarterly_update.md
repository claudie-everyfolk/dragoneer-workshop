# Q1 2026 Quarterly Portfolio Update

*Prepared for LP distribution — internal draft, not yet sent*

## Headline

Strong quarter on portfolio markups, paced deployment, and the first credible exit catalyst in the climate-tech book. DPI remains light; the team is actively pushing two portfolio companies toward realization events in H2 2026.

## Portfolio Performance Highlights

### Marks Up
- **Veridian AI** marked up to $4.2B following an oversubscribed Series D led by a strategic tier-1 buyer. Our entry basis remains at $1.1B; this is now the fund's largest unrealized gain and a candidate for partial secondary in H2.
- **Helix Health Systems** continues to compound — Q1 ARR +52% YoY, three new health-system contracts. Internal mark reflects the underlying KPI trajectory; we expect the deal team to revisit fair value at next valuation committee.
- **Stratus Logistics** crossed 138% NRR. Expansion is concentrated in the top 20 accounts; mid-market churn has improved.

### Marks Down
- **Arclight Robotics** marked down 22%. Enterprise sales cycles in industrial AI have lengthened materially across the sector. Company extended runway via insider round; we participated pro rata to maintain ownership.

### New Investments This Quarter
- **Northforge Energy** — APAC-headquartered carbon-capture platform; first deployment in Singapore. Led the Series B. First climate-tech position with material APAC exposure.

### Exit Activity
- **Solas Renewables** has initiated an S-1 process. Target IPO window is late 2026. If executed, this would be the fund's largest realization to date and meaningfully move DPI.

## Theme Exposures (% of NAV)

- AI Infrastructure: 24%
- Vertical SaaS: 22%
- Healthcare IT: 18%
- Climate Tech / Energy Transition: 12%
- Industrial AI / Robotics: 9%
- Other: 15%

## Deployment Pace

Deployed $185M across 3 new investments and 2 follow-ons in Q1. Pacing slightly behind plan; remaining dry powder of ~$420M against an 18-month deployment window. Pipeline is healthy across healthcare IT and climate tech; APAC sourcing is improving but still under-indexed relative to the LP base's stated preferences.

## ESG Reporting Note

Beginning this quarter, we are appending an ESG summary to all LP-facing materials, with company-level disclosures available on request. Northforge Energy is our first portfolio company with a primary climate-impact thesis; reporting framework is being finalized with the deal team.

## Outlook

H2 2026 should be the inflection on DPI. Two named realization candidates (Solas IPO, Veridian secondary) plus continued underlying compounding in the healthcare IT and vertical SaaS books. Risk watch: industrial AI exposure (Arclight read-across) and pace of APAC sourcing relative to LP expectations.
