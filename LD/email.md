Month-end always ends the same way: spend data in one system, vendor invoices in another, capital activity in a third, and you stitching it together into something the partners can actually read. The reconciliation isn't the hard part — the narrative is.

In our session you'll build a partner-ready monthly close brief in Claude Code. You'll point it at three synthetic files (spend vs. budget by GL category, vendor invoices, capital call/distribution activity) plus a short context note explaining the month's one-offs. Claude will produce a single-page brief with variance flags, vendor notes, capital activity summary, and a narrative on the top 3 variances. First usable output in ~10 minutes.

Prompt and sample data are attached. The synthetic data is calibrated to roughly the volume of a real month — not your real numbers, so feel free to push it.
