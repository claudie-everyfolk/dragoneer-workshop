# Q2 2026 — Firm-Wide Strategic Priorities

_Confidential — Leadership Team Only_
_Last refreshed: 2026-04-15 by President & COO_

## Top-Level Theme for Q2

Demonstrate disciplined deployment in Fund VI while protecting LP confidence through transparent communication on existing portfolio challenges. Operational posture is **steady-state with selective acceleration** — no major new initiatives, but no slowdown on commitments already in motion.

---

## Priority 1: Complete Fund VI Tranche 3 Capital Deployment (Investment)

- **Owner:** Helen K. (Head of Investment)
- **Definition of done:** Tranche 3 fully called and deployed across at least two new platform investments and one bolt-on by end of Q2.
- **Status check-in cadence:** Weekly via Monday huddle.
- **Known dependencies:** IR capital call notice timing; Compliance pre-clearance turnaround on related-party situations.
- **Risk areas:** Project Lattice timing slippage could push one platform investment into Q3. Watch closely.

## Priority 2: Stabilize Calderon Logistics Position (Investment + Finance)

- **Owners:** Marcus T. (deal partner), Sasha P. (Head of Finance) jointly.
- **Definition of done:** Either a credible operational turnaround plan in place by end of Q2, or a clean process initiated to evaluate alternatives.
- **Critical inputs:** Bank covenant analysis, restructuring options memo, LP communication strategy.
- **Risk areas:** Cornerstone LP and two other top-five LPs have flagged exposure concerns. Loss of LP confidence is the larger risk than the position itself.

## Priority 3: Maintain LP Trust Through Mid-Year Cycle (IR)

- **Owner:** Devon R. (Head of IR)

- **Critical inputs:** Coordinated messaging on Calderon Logistics across Devon, Marcus, and President.
- **Risk areas:** Uncoordinated messaging between deal team and IR. Cornerstone situation is the canary.

## Priority 4: Pass Spring Compliance Cycle Cleanly (Compliance)

- **Owner:** Yara M. (Chief Compliance Officer)
- **Definition of done:** ADV amendment filed (done), personal trading policy update ratified by board, marketing review backlog at zero by end of June, cybersecurity tabletop completed.
- **Critical inputs:** Board sub-committee scheduling, legal interpretations on related-party rules.
- **Risk areas:** Cybersecurity tabletop slippage to July creates a Q3 squeeze if anything else slips.

## Priority 5: Operational Leverage Without Headcount (Operations)

- **Owner:** President & COO
- **Definition of done:** Identify and pilot two operational workflows where AI tooling reduces coordination overhead, with explicit compliance-approved boundaries on data handling.
- **Critical inputs:** This priority is intentionally lighter — exploration phase, not delivery phase, for Q2.
- **Risk areas:** Initiative gets pushed by higher-urgency items above. Schedule explicit time blocks or it doesn't happen.
